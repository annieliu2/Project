Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 1/3 cups of overripe bananas
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 eggs beaten, room temp
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Grease a 9x5 in loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in one bowl. In another bowl, mix the butter and brown sugar.
  3. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas in the butter mixture.
  4. Combine both bowls and pour batter into pan
  5. Bake for 60-65 minutes and cool before serving
