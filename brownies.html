Walnut Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Combine the melted butter, sugar cocoa powder, vanilla, eggs, flour baking powder, and salt.
  3. Spread the batter into the pan and decorate with walnut halves on top.
  4. Bake in oven for 20-30 minutes and cool before serving.
