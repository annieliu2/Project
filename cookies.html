Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg, room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 4 oz of milk
  • 4 oz of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Whisk the sugars, salt, and butter in a bowl with no lumps
  3. Whisk the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short time before falling back into the mixture
  4. Sift the flour and baking soda, then fold into the mixture with a spatula. DON'T OVERMIX!
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips in the same way and refrigerate dough for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and scoop dough onto the paper and make sure the cookies are spread apart.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes and cool completely before serving
