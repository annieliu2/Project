NY Cheesecake

Ingredients

    For the Crust

  • 1 1/2 cup of graham cracker crumbs
  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt

    For the Filling

  • 32 oz cream cheese, room temp
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

    Special Equipment

  • 9-inch spring-form pan
  • aluminum foil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit and wrap the outside of the spring-form pan with aluminum foil covering any seams. Spray inside of pan with cooking spray.
  2. Crush the graham crackers and combine it with butter, sugar and salt. Press the crumbs onto the bottom of the pan and spread evenly. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Reduce oven temp to 325 degrees fahrenheit and set a pot of water to boil
  4. In the bowl of an electric mixer, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and flour on medium speed for about 1 minute, make sure the mixture is evenly combined. Add the vanilla, lemon zest, lemon juice, and salt and beat on low speed until combined. Add the eggs, ONE at a time, mixing on low speed until combined. Mix in the sour cream until batter is uniform. DO NOT OVERMIX.
  5. Set the spring-form ban in a large roasting pan. Pour batter on top of the crust. Pour the boiled water on the roasting pan. Bake cake for 90-105 minutes.
  6. Take the roasting pan out and set it for 45 minutes. Then remove the spring-form pan and throw out the foil. COver cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
